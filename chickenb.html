Microwave Crisper/Fryer
(Chicken Breast)

  • --Use CRISPER PAN--
    (Place Crisper on truntable.)
    (Only use plastic tongs to move.)
  • Grease Crisper Pan
    (Few drops Oil, smeared with paper towel.
    Or spray with cooking spray.)
  • 1. (Pre-heat) Touch CRISP, then enter 300 for 3min, touch START.
  • 2. Place chicken breasts directly on Crisper.
    (Best if cut/flattened to same size.)
  • 3. (Cook) Press CRISP.
    Enter time to cook, see chart below. Touch START.
