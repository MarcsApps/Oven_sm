Soften / melt

  • --Place food on turntable--
  • 1. Touch SOFTEN/MELT.
  • Choose 1-Melt or 2-Soften (or repeatedly press SOFTEN/MELT to cycle choices)
  • 2. Choose food type by pressing SOFTEN/MELT to view choices,
    or directly choose the food by number, see chart below.
  • 3. Use keypad to enter weight (or sticks of butter).
  • 4. Touch START
